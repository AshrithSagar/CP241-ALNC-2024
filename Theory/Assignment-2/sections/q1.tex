\section*{Problem 1}

Compute the mathematical expression for the solution of the following system:
\begin{equation*}
    \dot x(t)
    =
    \begin{bmatrix}
        \frac{-t}{1+t^2} & 1                 \\
        0                & \frac{-4t}{1+t^2}
    \end{bmatrix}
    x(t)
\end{equation*}
Assume initial condition \( x(0) = x_0 \).
What happens if \( t \rightarrow \infty \)?
Explain your answer.

\subsection*{Solution}

We have been given \( \dot x(t) = A(t) x(t) \), where
\begin{equation*}
    A(t)
    =
    \begin{bmatrix}
        \frac{-t}{1+t^2} & 1                 \\
        0                & \frac{-4t}{1+t^2}
    \end{bmatrix}
\end{equation*}
